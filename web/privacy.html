Privacy Policy for CSV Zero

Effective Date: February 26, 2025

Last Updated: February 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CSV Zero. This application provides a simple and efficient way to view and edit CSV files. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any personal information while using CSV Zero.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

CSV Zero does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app runs entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection to function. Specifically, we do not:

  • Collect personal data such as names, email addresses, or payment information.
  • Track your usage of the app.
  • Store or transmit any CSV file contents outside your local device.

3. Permissions & Data Usage

CSV Zero may request access to files stored on your device for the purpose of opening and editing CSV files. However, this access is limited to user-selected files and does not involve data collection or storage beyond your local device.

4. Third-Party Services

This application does not use third-party analytics, cloud services, or external APIs. All processing occurs locally on your device.

5. Security

Since CSV Zero does not collect or transmit user data, there are no external security risks related to data transmission. However, we recommend that users take standard precautions when handling sensitive files.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If any changes are made to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above. We encourage users to review this policy periodically.
